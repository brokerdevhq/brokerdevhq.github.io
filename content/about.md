# About BrokerDev

BrokerDev is a software development firm that builds tools for independent insurance brokers. We specialize in the systems brokers actually use — Applied Epic, legacy databases, intake forms — and we connect them in ways that reduce manual work.

## Why brokers?

Insurance brokerage runs on data that lives in too many places at once. Policy management systems, spreadsheets, email threads, handwritten notes. The software exists to fix this, but integrations are expensive, vendors move slowly, and most off-the-shelf tools weren't built with your workflow in mind.

We focus exclusively on this space so we don't have to relearn it on your dime.

## How we work

We're pragmatic about technology. We're comfortable with legacy systems and modern APIs in the same project, because that's the reality of brokerage software.

No rip-and-replace. We scope work tightly, deliver quickly, and build things that actually get used.
